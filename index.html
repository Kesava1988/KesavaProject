What are we?

The T Kesava Sreeram (TKS) and its subsidiaries are collectively known as TKS Hotels Resorts and Palaces and is recognised as one of Asia's largest and finest hotel company.

TKS (luxury full-service hotels, resorts and palaces) is our flagship brand for the world's most discerning travelers seeking authentic experiences given that luxury is a way of life to which they are accustomed. Spanning world-renowned landmarks, modern business hotels, idyllic beach resorts, authentic Rajput palaces and rustic safari lodges, each TKS hotel reinterprets the tradition of hospitality in a refreshingly modern way to create unique experiences and lifelong memories.
